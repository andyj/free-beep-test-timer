Free Beep Test

The beep test, also known as the shuttle run test, is a popular fitness assessment used to measure cardiovascular endurance. Participants run back and forth between two markers placed 20 meters apart, synchronizing their pace with a series of beeps.

This simple one-page web application lets you run a beep test without the hassle of paid apps, adverts, or restrictions. It's an easy, straightforward, user-friendly app with a clean style that includes buttons to start, end, and clear the test results. Specific audio files play during the test to guide you.

Find this project at https://github.com/andyj/free-beep-test-timer
