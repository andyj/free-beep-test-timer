Free Beep Test

The beep test, also known as the shuttle run test, is a popular fitness assessment used to measure cardiovascular endurance. Participants run back and forth between two markers placed 20 meters apart, synchronizing their pace with a series of beeps.

The test starts slowly but gradually increases in speed, challenging the participant's stamina and endurance. It is widely used in schools, sports teams, and military settings to evaluate aerobic fitness levels.

Our free 20m beep test app provides a simple, ad-free solution for conducting the test. It features a clear and easy-to-use interface with buttons to start, end, and clear the test results. The app plays specific audio cues during the test to guide participants through each level and shuttle, ensuring a seamless and effective fitness assessment experience. Whether you're an athlete, coach, or fitness enthusiast, this tool is designed to help you monitor and improve your aerobic fitness.
